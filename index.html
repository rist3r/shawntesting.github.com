About Me

I am thrilled you found me and can’t wait to get to know you! I’m Jacque- wifey for over a decade, mama to 2 wild little boys and our big old Basset Hound. Finding clothes that made me feel feminine, sophisticated and beautiful was life-changing for me *no exaggeration* and giving that feeling of confidence to other women is why I am here.

Small Town Life

We live in Sierra Vista, AZ. A small city about 70 miles Southeast of Tucson. While we thankfully have a Target, what I wouldn’t do for a drive-thru coffee shop, because you know, kids.

Coffee Lover

You will RARELY ever see me without an iced coffee in my hand or within reach! Please make sure to have almond milk creamer on hand for me, it’s my favorite. Whenever we go somewhere new, scouting out the best local coffee spot is the first thing I do.

Beauty Enthusiast

Watching YouTube makeup tutorials, browsing the Sephora website and building my makeup collection are what you can find me spending my free time doing. I love how a lip color changes the feel of an entire outfit! If I had the chance, I’d go to school to learn to be a makeup artist.

Nurturing Heart

The thing I do most innately is taking care of and caring for others. I am a trained Marriage and Family Therapist and spent many years in the field of behavioral health supporting children and families through their experiences with mental illness. I also volunteer my time teaching caregivers how to wear their babies in carriers as a Volunteer Babywearing Educator with Babywearing International of Southeastern AZ as babywearing was critical in building my relationships with my own babies and I want other people to feel that connection too.

Foodie

Cooking and sharing a meal with the people I love and care for is one of my favorite things to do! I am an equal opportunity lover of food as long as it doesn’t have mushrooms (I’m allergic). I usually cook for our little family 6 nights a week and love hosting big holiday meals!

Personal Style

If I had to choose 3 words to describe my personal style I would choose: feminine, classic and comfortable. My favorite patterns are romantic, saturated florals and a classic stripe. I love them most mixed together! My style icons: Kate Middleton and Megan Markle. They always look so elegant but not over the top.
